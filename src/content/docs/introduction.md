Welcome to our **HackTheBox Walkthroughs**, a repository dedicated to documenting security solutions and machine writeups.

This handbook catalogs the knowledge, methodologies, and tools used to solve various HackTheBox active labs, machines, and challenges over the years.

---

## 🎯 The Mission

Solving HackTheBox machines is a highly practical way to understand vulnerability vectors, host-based exploitation, and privilege escalation in a controlled sandbox. This documentation acts as a structured reference and post-exploitation guide:

*   **Quick Reference**: Revisit past steps, ports, and custom scripts used for specific boxes.
*   **Methodology Catalog**: Build a structured habit of scanning, enumerating, exploiting, and escalating privileges.
*   **Documentation Habits**: Share findings cleanly with the community to explain the "why" behind every step.
